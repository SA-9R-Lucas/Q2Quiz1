Foods that scream Japanese

Food Name History
Tempura Was created in thee 16th century, when Portuguese Jesuits brought the Western-style cooking method of coating foods with flour and frying
Sushi Even though known as a Japanese cuisine, sushi actually originated form China
Sashimi Sashimi was originally created as a way to preserve fish
Tokoyaki Was first popularized in Osaka, where a street vendor named Tomekichi Endo is credited with its invention in 1935
Gyudon Gyūdon is considered to be derived from gyūnabe (牛鍋), a beef hot pot originating in the Kantō region of eastern Japan
